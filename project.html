About the Program

Scoreazy's Counseling & Mentorship Program is a student-first initiative designed to offer personalized academic support and skill-based mentoring. Whether you're preparing for exams, exploring new career paths, or learning tech skills like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript our mentors are here to guide you, step by step. Through 1-on-1 sessions, students receive tailored advice, study planning, project help, and career guidance. Our goal is to make learning simpler, stress-free, and more effective by focusing on each student's individual strengths and needs.
